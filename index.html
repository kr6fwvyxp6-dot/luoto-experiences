The idea

I'm Otto and I've been sailing these waters since I was a kid. The Helsinki archipelago is my backyard, and on this trip I take you into the part of it that most visitors never reach.

We leave Vuosaari Marina on a small private boat and head out into the archipelago. I choose our destination based on the conditions of the day. On the island, we explore on foot, then sit down to a proper Finnish picnic enjoying some local seasonal produce. No rush.

This isn't just a tour. It's what I actually do on summer days, and you're invited.
